FAQs

What is CRO?

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) is the process of increasing the percentage of website visitors who complete a desired action (e.g., purchase, sign-up).

Why is CRO important?

CRO helps maximize website traffic efficiency, increasing sales and ROI without needing more visitors.

What are common CRO strategies?

A/B testing landing pages Improving website speed and mobile responsiveness Simplifying checkout Adding trust signals (e.g., reviews, security badges)

What are common CRO mistakes?

Testing too many changes at once Not gathering enough data Ignoring mobile optimization

What is a good conversion rate?

A 2-5% conversion rate is typical for e-commerce, but even small improvements can significantly boost revenue.
